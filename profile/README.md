# TrustCAV Project

Welcome to the official GitHub organization for **TrustCAV**, a research initiative based at **Carleton University**. This project is proudly supported by the **Natural Sciences and Engineering Research Council of Canada (NSERC)**.

---

### Overview
TrustCAV focuses on the development, testing, and validation of **Connected and Autonomous Vehicles (CAV)**. Our primary mission is to enhance the reliability and safety of autonomous systems through rigorous software engineering practices and innovative research.

### Core Objectives
* **Trustworthy Autonomy:** Researching methods to ensure CAV systems are secure, reliable, and predictable.
* **Verification & Validation:** Implementing robust automated testing and regression suites for autonomous software stacks.
* **Collaborative Research:** Providing a centralized hub for students, faculty, and industry partners to contribute to the future of transportation technology.

### Getting Started
If you are a member of the research team or a collaborator:

* **Explore the Repositories:** Check out our active projects ranging from perception modules to infrastructure simulation.
* **Documentation:** Refer to the `docs` folder within individual repositories for setup instructions and contribution guidelines.
* **Contributions:** Please follow our internal branching strategy and ensure all PRs pass the required CI/CD checks before merging.
